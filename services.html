Online E-Services

Digital Government Services

Access various government services online from the comfort of your home. The Government of Nepal is committed to providing efficient and transparent digital services to all citizens.

Citizen Services

  • Nagarikta Online - Apply for citizenship certificates online
  • Passport Services - Apply for new passports or renew existing ones
  • Birth/Death Registration - Register vital events online
  • Pan Card Registration - Apply for Permanent Account Number
  • Driving License - Apply for or renew driving licenses

Business Services

  • Company Registration - Register new companies and businesses
  • Tax Payment System - Pay taxes and file returns online
  • Import/Export Permits - Apply for trade licenses and permits
  • Patent and Trademark - Register intellectual property
  • VAT Registration - Register for Value Added Tax

Property Services

  • Land Registration - Register land and property transactions
  • Property Tax Payment - Pay property taxes online
  • Building Permits - Apply for construction permits
  • Lalpurja Services - Access land ownership documents

Educational Services

  • SLC/SEE Results - View and download exam results
  • Scholarship Applications - Apply for government scholarships
  • Character Certificate - Obtain character certificates
  • Transcript Services - Request academic transcripts

Health Services

  • Hospital Appointments - Book appointments at government hospitals
  • Health Insurance - Apply for health insurance schemes
  • Medical Records - Access personal health records
  • Vaccination Records - View vaccination history

Utility Services

  • Electricity Bill Payment - Pay NEA bills online
  • Water Bill Payment - Pay water supply bills
  • Telephone Bill Payment - Pay NTC/Ncell bills
  • Traffic Fine Payment - Pay traffic violation fines
