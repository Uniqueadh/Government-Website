Government Ministries

Federal Ministries of Nepal

The Government of Nepal operates through various ministries and departments dedicated to different sectors of national development and public service.

Core Ministries

  • Ministry of Home Affairs - Internal security, law and order, immigration
  • Ministry of Finance - Economic policy, budget, taxation, revenue
  • Ministry of Foreign Affairs - International relations and diplomacy
  • Ministry of Defense - National defense and security
  • Ministry of Education, Science and Technology - Education system and research

Development Ministries

  • Ministry of Health and Population - Public health services and population management
  • Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock Development - Agricultural development and food security
  • Ministry of Energy, Water Resources and Irrigation - Energy production and water management
  • Ministry of Industry, Commerce and Supplies - Industrial development and trade
  • Ministry of Physical Infrastructure and Transport - Roads, bridges, and transportation

Social Welfare Ministries

  • Ministry of Women, Children and Senior Citizens - Social welfare and protection
  • Ministry of Youth and Sports - Youth development and sports promotion
  • Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation - Cultural preservation and tourism
  • Ministry of Labour, Employment and Social Security - Labour rights and employment

Administrative Ministries

  • Ministry of Law, Justice and Parliamentary Affairs - Legal framework and justice
  • Ministry of Federal Affairs and General Administration - Federal coordination
  • Ministry of Communication and Information Technology - Digital infrastructure
  • Ministry of Land Management, Cooperatives and Poverty Alleviation - Land reform and poverty reduction
