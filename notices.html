Latest Government Notices and Announcements

Stay informed with the latest notices, announcements, and updates from various government departments and ministries.

Recent Notices - January 2025

Public Service Commission Examination Notice

Date: January 18, 2025

Applications are invited for various positions in the Nepal Administrative Service. Interested candidates must submit their applications by February 15, 2025.

Tax Filing Deadline Extension

Date: January 15, 2025

The deadline for filing annual tax returns has been extended to March 1, 2025. Taxpayers are requested to file their returns before the new deadline.

National Identity Card Distribution

Date: January 12, 2025

Citizens can now collect their National Identity Cards from their respective district administration offices. Please bring your citizenship certificate and recent passport-size photograph.

Important Announcements - December 2024

New Passport Fee Structure

Date: December 28, 2024

Updated passport fees are now in effect. Regular passport: NPR 5,000, Fast track: NPR 10,000. For details, visit the Department of Passports website.

Education Scholarship Program 2025

Date: December 20, 2024

The Ministry of Education announces scholarship opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students. Applications open from January 1, 2025.

COVID-19 Vaccination Drive

Date: December 15, 2024

Booster doses are now available at all government health centers. Citizens above 18 years are eligible for vaccination without prior appointment.
